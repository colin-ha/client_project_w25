UMSI Tutoring Services

Tutoring Services Available to SI Students

The University is committed to helping students to achieve their absolute best. We want to provide the best resources available to help you to learn and succeed in your classes. For this reason, we offer tutoring and academic support, available to all students.

Peer tutoring is available, both for technical programming tasks, and for mathematics. Additional academic resources may also be available. We hope that you will be able to find additional help in whatever you need. By engaging with our peer tutors, we hope that you will receive valuable assistance and support in your classes.

Accessing Tutoring Services

Visit the UMSI Tutoring Services website for more information. Note that you must be signed in to your umich.edu email address to access the page. For more information beyond this regarding student support and tutoring, contact umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu.
