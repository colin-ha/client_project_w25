Food Pantry

Maize and Blue Cupboard

The Maize and Blue Cupboard is a resource available to students who may be struggling with food insecurity or physical restraints that limit their access to food. The university believes it is the basic right of every person to be well-fed at all times, and that healthy eating is essential for success in and out of the classroom.

Accessing the Food Pantry

The food pantry is located in the basement of the Betsy Barbour residence hall. They ask that all students enter via the Maynard entrance to protect resident privacy. More information is available at their website.
