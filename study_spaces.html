Study Spaces

Available Locations on Campus

The University of Michigan wants to encourage students to spend valuable time working towards their education. For this reason, there are many quality and accessible study spaces available to students on campus. In many cases, reservations are not needed, and students are free to simply show up, and get to work. Often you will find resources to help you succeed, such as power supplies, computer stations, remote-class attendance spaces, and more.

Find a Study Space

The University publishes a list of study spaces on an interactive guide. In most cases, reservations are not required. There are also many more study spaces around campus than just the ones listed here, and in most cases, the University is more than happy to allow you to study wherever works best for you, as long as it doesn't interfere with other events.
